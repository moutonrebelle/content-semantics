Beetroot (Beta vulgaris)

  1. Common varieties

55 to 65 days are needed from germination to harvest of the rood

The usually deep purple roots of beetroot are eaten either grilled, boiled, or roasted as a cooked vegetable, cold as a salad after cooking or adding oil and vinegar, or raw and shredded, either alone or combined with any salad vegetable.

In fact, Juim Halpert once said this about beets, "Fact, bears eat beets. Bears, beets, Battlestar Galactica".

I have some weird habits. For instance, I love beets. Show me a salad bar and I will clean them out of their beets. -Chris Pratt
